Bed & Breakfast is a fully furnished historic home with 10 guest rooms and many spacious and inviting areas providing the charm of an old world inn. As the first and longest established guest house in the University of Minnesota area, we have hosted almost 4000 guests from over 67 countries, fostering a truly international community in this turn-of-the-century home. For the past 18 years, we have been the lodging of choice for visiting scholars, researchers, physicians and others for stays ranging from two nights to one year. Many of our guests return for more visits once they experience the friendly atmosphere, appealing spaces and great value for which Bed & Breakfast has become known.

Location


Unlike other lodging choices in the University of Minnesota area, Bed & Breakfast is not located on a busy, high-traffic street or intersection. There is very little traffic noise. The Historic District of 5th St. S.E. is a one lane, one-way street with a bike lane. The plentiful green space and majestic 125 year old oak trees lend calm and beauty to the area. Yet we are within easy walking distance of many of the area’s top attractions. Freeway and city street access is very convenient.

5 minute walk:

  • The University of Minnesota East Bank: a stunning campus full of interesting architecture, landscaping and other amenities. (The West Bank Campus is a 20 minute walk)
  • Dinkytown: a historic and pedestrian-friendly university village with unique shops and restaurants

20 minute walk:

  • The Stone Arch Bridge: Connecting Southeast Minneapolis with Downtown. A beautiful curved stone bridge crossing the Mississippi River once used by trains and now crossed by pedestrians and bikes only. Great views of downtown, St. Anthony Falls and the historic Mill district.
  • Mississippi River trails: Bike and walking trails go for miles on either side of the river.
  • East Hennepin shopping area: a variety of quality shops and restaurants
  • Historic 5th St. S.E. with its fine examples of classic architecture and a beautiful streetscape.

30 minute walk:

  • The world famous Guthrie Theater, Mill City Museum, Historic Riverfront and Nicollet Island
  • Downtown Minneapolis

Buses: Bus lines run 1 block from Bed & Breakfast allowing you to easily explore Downtown Minneapolis and St. Paul, The Walker Art Center/Loring Park area, Uptown Minneapolis and the lakes. The University of Minnesota Campus Circulator free shuttle bus runs 1 block away.
